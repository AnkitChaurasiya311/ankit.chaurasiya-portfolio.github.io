Ross lives in a small town with his father and son, he has a quiet and happy life. One day there is a hurricane that strikes the town that he lives in and his father goes missing. He informs the local rescue team that he’s gone missing. 1 week later he is told that they have been unable to find any trace of his father.

Whilst searching rubble near his house he finds a watch that has a counter on it that starts at “001”. Ross has a flashback that he previously found the same watch in the same place but it had a value of “000” and he pressed one of the buttons on the watch. He quickly realises that the watch is a time travelling watch.

With the new found watch he decides to take matters into his own hands and search for his father. Ross uses the watch to go back to when his father went missing to find out where he is. Unsure whether his father is alive or dead during the present, he begins heading out of town to where his father is.

With the use of the watch and the flashbacks that it gives him, he avoids numerous potential threats on the journey to find his missing father. In the end he finds his father and carries him to a hospital where he is treated.
